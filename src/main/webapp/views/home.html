WHAT WE DO

Australian CHD Research Consortium brings you "Register to be Registered" For the first time ever there are now more adults than Children living with a congenital heart condition. The fantastic success in surgery of the last two decades give us hope and we need to understand more about you now more than ever before! More...

HOW YOU CAN HELP

Improved surgical techniques have allowed fantastic survival and Quality of Life for most CHD patients. Therefore, we are now finding MORE ADULTS living with CHD that ever in history.
